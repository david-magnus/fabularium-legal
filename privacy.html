Privacy Policy — Fabularium Tales

Last updated: 3 July 2026

Fabularium Tales ("we", "us") operates a content channel that publishes narrated public-domain fables illustrated with 19th-century-style engravings, together with ambient study-music videos. This policy explains how our internal publishing tool handles data when it posts our own videos to third-party platforms such as TikTok, YouTube, Instagram and Threads.

What data we access

Our publishing tool uses official platform APIs only to upload videos we own to our own accounts. Through the platform's authorization flow we receive an access token tied to our own channel. We do not collect, store or process data belonging to viewers or other users.

How we use it

The access token is used solely to upload our videos and their metadata (title, description, tags, thumbnail) to our own accounts. Tokens are stored locally on our own machine, are never shared with third parties, and are never used to read other users' private data.

Data retention

Authorization tokens are kept locally only for as long as needed to publish content and can be revoked at any time from the platform's settings. We keep no viewer analytics beyond what the platforms themselves provide in their standard dashboards.

Third parties

We do not sell, rent or share any data. The only third parties involved are the publishing platforms themselves (TikTok, YouTube, Meta), each governed by their own privacy policies.
